My City: MADHUBANI

Tital:-The Timeless Beauty of Madhubani Art: A Cultural Gem of India

Madhubani art, also known as Mithila painting, is a traditional Indian folk art form that traces its roots to the Mithila region of Bihar. Renowned for its intricate patterns, vivid colors, and deep cultural symbolism, this art has transcended centuries, retaining its relevance and appeal in the modern world.

Origins and Tradition
